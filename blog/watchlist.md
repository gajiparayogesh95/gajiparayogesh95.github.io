## What Is This List?

These are businesses I find interesting enough to track but where I either haven't completed a full analysis, haven't found an attractive entry price, or am waiting for more clarity on a specific risk factor.

Being on the watchlist does **not** mean I own the stock or that it is currently attractive. It means the business meets enough of my initial criteria to deserve ongoing attention.

## How Companies Leave the Watchlist

A company graduates off this list in one of three ways:

1. **Full analysis published** — I complete a write-up and either initiate a position or pass with documented reasoning
2. **Thesis disconfirmed** — Something I learn during research disqualifies the business from further consideration
3. **Valuation no longer compelling** — Price has moved well above my estimate of intrinsic value before I had a chance to act
